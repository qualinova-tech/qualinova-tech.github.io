Our QA Process

Structured Testing Workflow for Reliable Delivery

Our QA workflow is designed to help teams move faster with clarity, coverage and confidence — from requirement analysis to final release validation.

01

Requirement Analysis

We understand the product, business flow, testing scope, environments and critical user journeys before starting QA activities.

02

Test Planning

We define the testing approach, test coverage, timelines, environments and whether manual or automation testing is best suited.

03

Scenario Preparation

We prepare test scenarios, edge cases, regression coverage and validation checklists aligned to your product workflows.

04

Manual / Automation Execution

Our team executes manual tests or automation suites using proven tools and structured QA practices for consistent results.

05

Defect Reporting

We report issues clearly with reproduction steps, screenshots, severity details and observations to speed up developer resolution.

06

Retesting & Validation

After fixes are delivered, we retest issues, validate impacted workflows and confirm release readiness with confidence.
